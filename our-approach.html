Hybrid intelligence

Power of technology + power of people

The methodology of QuantumBlack, AI by McKinsey, harnesses the power of AI combined with human insight. It blends the agility of multidisciplinary teams, the deepest domain expertise with cutting-edge AI, and the strength of McKinsey’s industry understanding.

This balance unlocks hybrid intelligence—the competitive advantage that helps organizations innovate and thrive.

Our Guiding Principles

The foundation of our work

01

Harmonize humans and AI

We harness the power of technology and people to create growth that benefits organizations while supporting a thriving, sustainable, and inclusive society.

02

Leverage industry expertise

Our deep machine-learning and AI expertise helps us make sense of complex data faster, pinpoint where the greatest value lies, drive adoption, and scale the solution.

03

Accelerate impact

We hit the ground running. Our operating protocols, proprietary tools, and experienced teams help our clients prove value from day one and realize it quickly.

04

Build confidence and trust

We build confidence that our AI solutions are socially responsible, ethical, and legal, by being clear about where the insights come from.

05

Build with clients, not for them

We’re transformation partners working alongside our clients to embed new platforms and give teams the capabilities they need to thrive long after we’re gone.

06

Create change that sticks

We partner with you to design your future-state organization, hire for new roles, and build capabilities across business and technology, coaching you along the way.

07

Focus on needs before technology

Our technology-agnostic approach means we’re focused on creating the best solution for each situation—and are equally at ease building from scratch or working with existing infrastructure.

08

Assemble diverse teams

Designing AI solutions to deliver business impact requires diverse teams that integrate business expertise and technological depth. We bring the right skills, at the right time.

09

Embrace innovation as the only constant

We invest in advancing technology frontiers and applying the latest learnings. By donating Kedro, we aim to learn more, together with the community.
